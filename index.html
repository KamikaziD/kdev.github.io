Welcome to Habitat


Habitat is a self-catering cottage that is situated at the end of a peaceful cul-de-sac in the heart of Clarens. The house is beautifully decorated to enhance your country experience and comfortably sleeps 5 guests in 3 bedrooms.

The main bedroom is furnished with a king-size bed, the guest bedroom is furnished with a queen-size bed and the third bedroom is furnished with a sofa bed. The main en-suite bathroom is fitted with a shower and a bath while the guest bathroom has a shower only.

There is also a large open-plan living area with a wood-burning fireplace and a fully equipped kitchen. Children of all ages are welcome but are at all times under full parental responsibility. Breakfast is optional, served in the form of a continental basket delivered to your door.

English breakfast is available on request, offered at the nearest restaurant which is walking distance from the property. All breakfast options are not included in the room rate. Kindly inquire for more information.

Enjoy the quiet and peaceful setting of Habitat


A patio leads out from the living room into a tranquil garden area overlooking the mountains. Additional amenities include complimentary wi-fi, tea and coffee facilities, a welcome drink, fireplace and barbeque facilities. Secure undercover parking on site available for your convenience.


Activities & More


Habitat is a serene retreat nestled in a lovely private garden with fabulous views. It is centrally located from restaurants, galleries and shops. Nearby attractions include hiking, mountain biking, bird watching, skiing (seasonal), locally produced cheese & wine tastings and many more.
